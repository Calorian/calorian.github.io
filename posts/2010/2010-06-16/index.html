Moo Rebuild - Part 01

16 Jun 2010

Setting the scene.

So the Dolomite, codename “Moo” is a 1979 1850HL model in Vermillion but sadly she is in need of some serious TLC. I’ve a pot of money and a mission of fixing it up and making the car look stunning again. This will be my first proper big classic car job but I am looking forward to the challenge and hopefully it’ll be fun.

Honestly, I’m not really the most organized person in the world but I’m going to try and document as much of the process as I can in order to show what I’ve been up to and so I can remember for the future! I have a little budget sheet made up, which I’ll link to later and also I’m sure if you keep an eye on these posts parts may go up for sale as I acquire spares over the course of the job.
